Purpose

The summer assignment introduces the core habits of AP Microeconomics: reading carefully, defining terms precisely, applying models, and explaining choices with evidence. You should arrive ready to discuss, ask questions, and use the vocabulary of the course.

Expected Workload

Plan for steady, focused work across the summer rather than one rushed sitting. Most students should expect several short study sessions per chapter, plus time to review vocabulary and prepare the final submission.

Assignment Chapters

Chapter 1 The Economic Way of Thinking Scarcity, incentives, marginal thinking Chapter 2 Opportunity Cost, Tradeoffs, and the PPF Models, efficiency, economic growth Chapter 3 Comparative Advantage and Gains from Trade Specialization, opportunity cost, trade logic Chapter 4 Intro to Supply and Demand Markets, equilibrium, shifts

How to Use This Site

  • Complete each chapter page in order.
  • Keep a neat notebook or digital document with vocabulary, notes, and responses.
  • Use the vocabulary page to review, quiz yourself, and study responsibly.
  • Use the final checklist before submitting your work.
