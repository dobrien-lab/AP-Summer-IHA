Lilly D and the PPF

Use this comic to review how the Production Possibilities Frontier shows scarcity, tradeoffs, opportunity cost, efficiency, inefficiency, impossible points, and economic growth.

Back to Chapter 2

What to Notice

  • The PPF shows the best possible combinations an economy can produce.
  • Moving along the curve means making a tradeoff.
  • Producing more of one good means giving up some of another good.
  • Points inside the curve are possible but inefficient.
  • Points outside the curve are impossible with current resources and technology.
  • An outward shift of the PPF shows economic growth.
